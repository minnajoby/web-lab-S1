About Us

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology, named after the late Prime Minister Sri Rajiv Gandhi, run by the Government of Kerala, started functioning in 1991.

Courses offered

The B. Tech programmes are of 8 semester (4 year) duration. The B. Arch programme is of 10 semester (5 year) duration. The M. Tech programmes are of 4 semester (2 year) duration, and the MCA programme is of 4 semester (2 year) duration.
